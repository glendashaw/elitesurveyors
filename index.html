Our Services

NATURAL GAS LEAK DETECTION SURVEYING

Walking residential areas to check for gas leaks along pipes, gas meters and covers. After the walking residential surveys, the contractor will begin surveying natural gas service points, mains and meters in business districts. This type of surveying in the business districts includes atmospheric test on the meter near gas mains, in utility manholes, at pavement and sidewalk cracks, and other locations in which it is possible to identify gas leaks.

UTILITY METER PAINTING

Safely drive to scheduled customer locations and hand-paint meters with approved paint. We also take the initiative to report any observed conditions that seem unsafe or unusual.
